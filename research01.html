Cannabinoid's role in oral health

New research on oral health care.

Informations:

  • The cannabinoids infused mouthwash merchandise confirmed the same bactericidal efficacy as that of chlorhexidine 0.2%. Both chlorhexidine 0.2% and cannabinoids infused mouthwash merchandise dominated as opposed to all the tested samples.

    • The highlighted findings were:

  • Product A no longer displayed any sizable antimicrobial body in any of the samples tested.
  • Product B no longer displayed any detectable inhibition area in any respect in any of the samples tested.

    • The tiers for inhibition zones showed the following results:

  • For CBD mouthwash, the tiers inhibition zones have been 8–25 mm (18.1 mm).
  • CBD-mouthwash, 8–25 mm (17.7 mm) for CBG-mouthwash; 12–25 mm (16.8 mm).
  • For chlorhexidine 0.2%; 0–8 mm (0.1 mm) for Product A; and 0 mm for Product B.
  • Although the distinction in performance changed to barely better than chlorhexidine in each case, the distinction changed into statistically sizable for CBD-mouthwash and close to sizable for CBG-mouthwash.
